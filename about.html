About Storytime App

The Storytime App is designed to make reading with your child interactive and engaging. Our goal is to help parents and children enjoy storytime together with features like real-time word highlighting and AI-assisted reading.

Our Mission

We believe that every child deserves a magical reading experience. By combining technology with storytelling, we aim to foster a love for reading and learning in young minds.
